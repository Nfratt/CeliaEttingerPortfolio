About Me

Celia, a Bay Area native, moved to New York after hearing Jay-Z proclaim, “If you can make it here, you can make it anywhere.” During her time at Badger & Winters, she has produced broadcast, social, print, digital and experiential assets for clients like JCPenney, Olay, Beautyrest, Dick's Sporting Goods and others.

In her free time, Celia enjoys sampling the finest foods the city has to offer, expanding her ever growing list of must-try restaurants. She likes to consider herself something of an amateur food connoisseur. Somewhere, under the close supervision of Beyonce, Jay-Z is smiling down on Celia's past success, and bright future.
